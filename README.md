# Hi, I'm Ali Mahmoud Ahmed Ibrahim

### Biomedical Engineering Student | Software, AI & Embedded Healthcare Systems

I am a third-year Biomedical Engineering student at Cairo University, building projects at the intersection of **medical software**, **AI/data**, **embedded systems**, **medical devices**, and **digital health**.

My work focuses on turning biomedical and healthcare problems into functional engineering solutions — from medical image processing apps and hospital information systems to EMG-controlled prosthetics and STM32-based embedded systems.
